Who we are

Axceltrees is a premier executive search and recruiting firm headquartered in Bangalore, India. It is promoted by an industry veteran with more than 30+ years of experience and employs very talented staff who are in the field of Human Resources for more than fifteen years.

Axceltrees has consulted with close to 300 organizations and have placed a little over 3,000 professionals with various clients.

Why us

When you consult with Axceltrees, you can be assured that our search consultants with deep knowledge and extensive experience in your industry are committed to helping your organization identify and attract the best talent.

Axceltrees does not charge any professional fee whatsoever for candidates availing our assistance in placements.
